<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold dark:text-gray-100 text-gray-900 mb-4">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base dark:text-gray-400 text-gray-600 mb-8">Get up and running with HoYoVista in minutes</p>
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Add HoYoVista to your Discord">
      Invite the bot to your Discord server or use it directly in DMs. HoYoVista supports both server and personal interactions.
      
      <Note>
        HoYoVista uses Discord's slash commands, so make sure the bot has permission to use application commands in your server.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Register your account">
      Run the `/register` command to create your HoYoVista account. You'll need to agree to the privacy policy and terms of service.
      
      ```bash
      /register
      ```
      
      This creates your account and enables you to use all bot features.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Link your HoYoLAB account">
      Use the `/hoyolink` command to connect your HoYoLAB account to Discord. This allows HoYoVista to access your game data and perform actions on your behalf.
      
      ```bash
      /hoyolink
      ```
      
      <Warning>
        Keep your HoYoLAB cookies secure. Never share them with anyone. HoYoVista stores them encrypted in the database.
      </Warning>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Start using commands">
      You're all set! Try checking in for daily rewards:
      
      ```bash
      /check-in
      ```
      
      Explore other commands like `/profile`, `/notes`, and `/settings` to customize your experience.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
