Welcome to HoYoVista! This guide will help you set up the bot and perform your first daily check-in in just a few minutes.

## What is HoYoVista?

HoYoVista is a Discord bot that brings HoYoLAB features directly to Discord. Access daily check-ins, view game profiles, track income, and more - all without leaving your Discord server.

**Supported Games:**
- Genshin Impact
- Honkai: Star Rail
- Honkai Impact 3rd
- Zenless Zone Zero
- Tears of Themis

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Your Account">
    Start by registering your Discord account with HoYoVista:

    ```bash
    /register
    ```

    You'll be prompted to agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Click **"Agree and Register"** to continue.

    <Note>
      Registration is required before you can use any HoYoVista features. This creates your account profile in the bot's database.
    </Note>

    After successful registration, you'll see available commands:
    - `/hoyolink` - Link/unlink your HoYoLAB account
    - `/settings` - Access and modify your account settings
    - `/data` - Manage your data, including viewing or deleting it
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link Your HoYoLAB Account">
    Connect your HoYoLAB account to enable all bot features:

    ```bash
    /hoyolink
    ```

    Click **"Add HoYoLAB Account"** and you'll be prompted to enter your HoYoLAB cookies.

    <Accordion title="How do I get my HoYoLAB cookies?">
      Click the info button (ℹ️) next to "Add HoYoLAB Account" for detailed instructions, or follow these steps:

      1. Go to [HoYoLAB](https://www.hoyolab.com/home) and log in
      2. Click your profile picture and select **"Personal Homepage"**
      3. Open your browser's DevTools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
      4. Navigate to the **"Network"** tab and refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
      5. Type **"getGame"** in the filter box
      6. Click on **"getGameRecordCard"**
      7. Under **"Request Headers"**, find the `Cookie` field
      8. Copy everything after **"Cookie:"**
      9. Paste into the bot's cookie field

      <Warning>
        Keep your cookies private! They provide access to your HoYoLAB account. Never share them with anyone else.
      </Warning>

      **Example cookie format:**
      ```
      ltmid_v2=____; ltoken_v2=v2____; ltuid_v2=____; DEVICEFP=____; account_id_v2=____; cookie_token_v2=v2____
      ```

      Required cookies: `ltmid_v2`, `ltoken_v2`, `ltuid_v2`
    </Accordion>

    Once your cookies are validated, the bot will automatically detect all games linked to your HoYoLAB account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Perform Your First Check-In">
    Claim your daily HoYoLAB rewards directly from Discord:

    ```bash
    /check-in
    ```

    **Check in for all games at once:**
    ```bash
    /check-in
    ```
    Leave the `account` option empty to check in all linked games simultaneously.

    **Check in for a specific game:**
    ```bash
    /check-in account:Genshin Impact
    ```
    Select from your linked games using autocomplete.

    <Note>
      The `/check-in` command has a 30-minute cooldown to prevent rate limiting from HoYoLAB servers.
    </Note>

    You'll receive a summary showing:
    - Game name and UID (censored if privacy mode is enabled)
    - Reward received (item name and quantity)
    - Number of missed check-in days
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Additional Features">
    Now that you're set up, try these commands:

    **View Game Profile:**
    ```bash
    /profile account:Genshin Impact
    ```
    Displays detailed stats, achievements, and game progress.

    **Track Income:**
    ```bash
    /income
    ```
    View monthly currency income from supported games.

    **Manage Settings:**
    ```bash
    /settings
    ```
    Configure privacy settings, data collection preferences, and more.

    **View Roadmap:**
    ```bash
    /roadmap
    ```
    See planned features and improvements.
  </Step>
</Steps>
