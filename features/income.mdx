The `/income` command provides detailed reports on your monthly currency income, showing how much you've earned and where it came from.

## Usage

```bash
/income account:<game> month:<month>
```text

### Parameters

- `account` (required) - The game account to view income for
- `month` (required) - The month to view (current, previous, or two months ago)

### Examples

**Check current month income:**
```bash
/income account:Genshin Impact month:March
```text

**Check previous month:**
```bash
/income account:Zenless Zone Zero month:February
```text

<Info>
  The income command has a 30-second cooldown per user.
</Info>

## How It Works

When you request an income report:

1. HoYoVista fetches your Traveler's Diary/ledger from HoYoLAB
2. Analyzes your currency income for the selected month
3. Breaks down income by source category
4. Identifies your primary income source
5. Compares with previous month (if applicable)

## Month Selection

The month autocomplete shows the last 3 months:

- Current month
- Previous month (last month)
- Two months ago

Months are displayed by name (January, February, etc.) and internally mapped to month numbers (1-12).

### Genshin Impact

**Daily Commissions:** Regular daily quest rewards

**Events:** Limited-time event primogems

**Spiral Abyss:** Floor 9-12 completion rewards

## Income Optimization Tips

### Maximize Your Earnings

1. **Complete dailies every day** - Consistent daily income
2. **Clear all event content** - Events are the largest source
3. **Full-star Abyss/Shiyu** - Don't miss bi-weekly rewards
4. **Finish story quests** - One-time primogem rewards
5. **Explore thoroughly** - Chests add up over time

### Track Your Progress

Use the income command to:
- Set monthly earning goals
- Identify which content you're missing
- Compare month-over-month performance
- Plan for upcoming banners

## Data Accuracy

<Warning>
  Income data is delayed by 1-2 hours due to HoYoLAB caching. Recent earnings may not appear immediately.
</Warning>

**Update frequency:**
- Genshin Impact: ~1 hour delay
- Zenless Zone Zero: ~2 hours delay

**What's included:**
- All free-to-play currency sources
- Event rewards
- Daily/weekly content
- Quest rewards
- Mail and compensation

**What's excluded:**
- Top-up purchases
- Welkin Moon/Battle Pass (except free BP rewards)
- Currency conversions (e.g., Monochromes → Polychromes)

## Privacy & Censoring

Your UID is automatically censored in income reports based on privacy settings.

Modify privacy with:
```bash
/settings privacy:true
```text

## Troubleshooting

### No Data Available

- Check that you've earned currency in the selected month
- Verify your account was active during that period
- Some months may have zero income if inactive

### Old Data Showing

- Income data updates every 1-2 hours
- Today's earnings may not be included yet
- Try again after the next HoYoLAB update cycle

### API Error -100 (Star Rail)

This is a known limitation:
- Star Rail ledger API is currently unavailable
- HoYoverse may enable it in future updates
- Use the in-game tools for now
