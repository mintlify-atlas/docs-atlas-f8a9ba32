## What is Automation?

HoYoVista's automation features help you stay on top of daily rewards and limited-time codes without manual intervention. The bot can automatically perform check-ins and redeem codes across all your linked HoYoverse games.

## How It Works

Automation runs on a scheduled basis using cron jobs that execute tasks at specific times each day. When enabled, the bot will:

1. **Check for eligible accounts** - Only processes games where automation is enabled
2. **Execute the task** - Performs check-ins or redeems codes on your behalf
3. **Send notifications** - DMs you a summary of the results (if notifications are enabled)
4. **Handle errors gracefully** - Automatically disables automation if credentials become invalid

<Note>
All automation tasks include a random delay to distribute load and avoid rate limiting from HoYoLAB servers.
</Note>

## Supported Games

Automation is available for the following HoYoverse games:

- **Genshin Impact** - Full check-in and redemption support
- **Honkai: Star Rail** - Full check-in and redemption support
- **Zenless Zone Zero** - Full check-in and redemption support
- **Honkai Impact 3rd** - Check-in support; redemption requires manual in-game entry

<Warning>
Honkai Impact 3rd codes cannot be automatically redeemed via API. The bot will send you codes to redeem manually in-game.
</Warning>

## Privacy & Security

Your account security is our top priority:

- **Encrypted storage** - Your HoYoLAB cookies are stored securely
- **Read-only access** - The bot can only claim rewards, not modify your account
- **Automatic safeguards** - Invalid credentials automatically disable automation
- **Privacy controls** - Enable Privacy Blur in settings to hide UIDs in notifications

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Link your HoYoLAB account">
    Use `/register` to connect your HoYoLAB account and link your games
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure automation settings">
    Use `/settings` to enable auto check-in and auto redeem for each game
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable notifications">
    Make sure DM notifications are enabled so you receive summaries
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sit back and relax">
    The bot will handle your daily tasks automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Automation

You have full control over automation settings:

- **Per-game control** - Enable or disable automation for each linked game individually
- **Notification preferences** - Choose whether to receive DMs for each automation type
- **Easy toggles** - Use `/settings` to quickly enable or disable features

<Tip>
You can disable notifications but keep automation enabled if you prefer to check results manually.
</Tip>
